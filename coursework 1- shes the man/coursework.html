Synopsis & Pictures from the film

Viola Johnson (played by Amanda Bynes) is mad when the girls football team gets cut from her school due to budget cuts. She decides to pose as her twin brother Sebastian (played by James Kirk) and take his place at boarding school. However, Viola falls in love with her roommate Duke (played by Channing Tatum) however he is in love with his forever crush Oliva (played by Laura Ramsey). After Viola got second string in the tryouts, Viola and Duke come up with a plan to help her make first string and Viola to help Duke finally go out with Oliva.
